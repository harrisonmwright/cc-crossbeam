# Vertical Mixed Use (VMU)

## At a Glance
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| LDC Section | §25-2-586 |
| Ordinance | No. 20060216-Z-11 (original); significantly amended Ord. 20220929-175 (VMU2) |
| Incentive Type | Density Bonus (combining district) |
| Applicability | Lots in commercial base districts (CS, CS-1, GR, GO, LO, MF-3, MF-4, MF-5, MF-6) with -V combining district on corridors designated VMU in the Imagine Austin plan and approved City Council map |

## Incentives Granted
| Incentive | Standard Value | Bonus Value |
|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| Maximum height | Base district limit | Up to 60 ft (VMU1); up to 90 ft (VMU2 — additional affordability required) |
| FAR | Base district limit | Waived |
| Ground floor use | Commercial optional | Ground floor commercial required (residential not permitted at ground floor street frontage) |
| Setbacks | Base district minimums | Front setback waived along designated street frontage; side/rear per base district |
| Parking | Base district requirement | Waived for residential uses; commercial parking still required per §25-6 |

## Affordability Requirements

**VMU1 (up to 60 ft):**
| Parameter | Rental | Ownership |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Set-aside % | 10% of residential units | 10% of residential units |
| Max income limit | ≤80% MFI | ≤80% MFI |
| Affordability period | 40 years | 40 years |
| Fee-in-lieu | Available at Council-set rate |

**VMU2 (60–90 ft — additional requirement on top of VMU1):**
| Parameter | Rental | Ownership |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Additional set-aside | +5% of residential units (total 15%) | +5% (total 15%) |
| Max income limit for additional units | ≤60% MFI | ≤60% MFI |
| Base units (10%) | ≤80% MFI | ≤80% MFI |

**Math check (VMU1):** `affordable_units / residential_units ≥ 0.10`
**Math check (VMU2):** At least 10% at ≤80% MFI AND at least 5% at ≤60% MFI (total ≥ 15%)

## Dimensional Standards Under Bonus
| Parameter | VMU1 | VMU2 |
|-----------|------|------|
| Maximum height | 60 ft | 90 ft |
| Maximum FAR | Waived | Waived |
| Ground floor | Commercial/retail required at street frontage | Same |
| Front setback | Waived along VMU street frontage | Same |
| Side setback | Base district | Base district |
| Rear setback | Base district | Base district |
| Residential parking | Waived | Waived |
| Commercial parking | Per §25-6 table | Per §25-6 table |

## Required Plan Elements
1. **Cover sheet:** "VMU" or "VMU2" stated; LDC §25-2-586 cited; -V combining district shown in zoning designation
2. **Project data table:** Total residential units, affordable units, set-aside %, MFI tier, affordability period, ground floor commercial sq ft
3. **Affordability schedule:** Each affordable unit by ID, type, MFI tier, period
4. **Site plan:** Building footprint; street frontage labeled; height dimension shown; setbacks labeled (note front setback waiver if applicable)
5. **Ground floor plan:** Commercial/retail use shown at street frontage; no residential units at ground floor facing designated VMU street
6. **Floor plans:** All floors; affordable units labeled; residential above ground floor confirmed
7. **Elevations:** Maximum height from grade; story count; ground floor commercial fenestration/transparency shown

## Key Compliance Checks
- [ ] Zoning designation includes -V combining district
- [ ] VMU1 or VMU2 tier declared on cover sheet
- [ ] Ground floor shows commercial use at VMU street frontage (no residential at grade)
- [ ] Height ≤ 60 ft (VMU1) or ≤ 90 ft (VMU2)
- [ ] Affordable count ≥ 10% of residential units (VMU1) or ≥ 15% split across MFI tiers (VMU2)
- [ ] Income limit: ≤80% MFI (VMU1); ≤80% and ≤60% MFI tiers separately identified (VMU2)
- [ ] Affordability period ≥ 40 years
- [ ] Residential parking: waiver claimed or no parking spaces counted for residential
- [ ] Commercial parking provided per §25-6 (not waived)
- [ ] If fee-in-lieu: documentation present (do not compute fee amount)

## Notes
- VMU applies only on properties shown on the City Council-adopted VMU map; properties not on the map cannot invoke §25-2-586 — the skill trusts user declaration
- Ground floor commercial minimum depth requirement: commercial space must extend the full width of the building along the designated frontage and have a minimum depth of 20 ft (§25-2-586(C)(2))
- Fenestration requirement: ground floor must have ≥60% glazed area on the street-facing facade
- Parking waiver for residential applies only to spaces that would be required under §25-6 for residential uses; any parking provided voluntarily must still meet design standards
- VMU2 requires a Community Benefits Agreement (CBA) to be recorded before site plan approval
- VMU2 is available only on lots that are within the VMU overlay and on corridors where VMU2 height is shown on the adopted corridor plan
