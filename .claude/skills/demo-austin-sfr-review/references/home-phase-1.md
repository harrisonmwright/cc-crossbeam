# Austin HOME Initiative — Phase I

**Ordinance:** No. 20231207-001
**Adopted:** December 7, 2023
**Effective:** December 18, 2023
**Applies to:** Applications filed on or after February 5, 2024

---

## Summary

HOME Phase I allows up to three dwelling units on any SF-1, SF-2, or SF-3 zoned lot by right, eliminating single-family exclusivity. It adds "Three-Unit Residential" and renames "Two-Family Residential" to "Two-Unit Residential." It also raises the Multifamily threshold from 3 to 4+ units, repeals occupancy limits, and removes Family/Group Home civic uses.

---

## Allowable Unit Count

- **SF-1, SF-2, SF-3**: Up to **3 dwelling units per lot** (by right)
  - Duplex Residential: Permitted (P)
  - Two-Unit Residential: Permitted (P)
  - Three-Unit Residential: Permitted (P)
- **Multifamily** now defined as 4+ units (previously 3+), so 4+ units requires MF zoning

---

## New Definitions (§25-1-21)

- **Tiny Home**: A dwelling unit ≤400 sq ft in floor area, excluding loft space
- **Three-Unit Residential**: Use of a lot for three dwelling units (not mobile homes)
- **Two-Unit Residential**: Use of a lot for two dwelling units in separate buildings (not mobile homes)

---

## Development Standards for Duplex, Two-Unit, and Three-Unit Residential (§25-2-773)

These standards **supersede base zoning district regulations** to the extent of conflict.

### Lot and Setback Requirements
| Standard | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Minimum lot area | 5,750 sq ft |
| Minimum front yard setback | 15 ft |
| Minimum rear yard setback | Base district minimum, OR 5 ft if adjacent to an alley OR a lot permitted for multifamily or less restrictive use |
| Minimum street-side setback (corner lot, Level 1 street) | Greater of 5 ft from property line or 10 ft from curb |
| Minimum street-side setback (corner lot, Level 2–4 street) | 10 ft from property line |

### Coverage
| Standard | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Maximum building coverage | 40% |
| Maximum impervious cover | 45% |
| Maximum front yard impervious cover | 40% |

### Parking
- Max 4 parking spaces in front or street yard (combined for corner lots)
- Garage placement: Parking structure may not be closer to front lot line than the building facade's front-most exterior wall
- If garage entrance faces front yard and is <20 ft behind building facade, garage width may not exceed building facade width

### Minimum Entrances
- Minimum 1 street-facing entrance required

---

## Floor-to-Area Ratio (FAR) — Subchapter F Compatibility Area (§25-2-773(E))

### Duplex or Two-Unit Residential
- **Site maximum FAR**: Greater of **0.55** or **3,200 sq ft**
- **Per unit maximum**: Greater of **0.4** or **2,300 sq ft**

### Three-Unit Residential
- **Site maximum FAR**: Greater of **0.65** or **4,350 sq ft**
- **Per unit maximum**: Greater of **0.4** or **2,300 sq ft**
- **Two units combined maximum**: Greater of **0.55** or **3,200 sq ft**

---

## Preservation Bonus (§25-2-773(F))

Applicants who preserve an existing dwelling unit may exclude preserved sq footage from gross floor area calculations.

**For structures built on or before December 31, 1960:**
- Must preserve at least 50% of existing unit AND 100% of street-facing facade
- Alterations limited to area behind roof ridgeline/peak (side-gabled/cross-gabled/hipped/pyramidal) or to lesser of 15 ft from front facade or half the front wall width (front-gabled/shed/flat)
- Historic landmark rules supersede if applicable

**For structures built on or after January 1, 1961, and at least 20 years old:**
- Must preserve at least 50% of existing unit

---

## Short-Term Rental Restrictions (§25-2-773(D))

- Applies to duplex and two-unit residential uses constructed after October 1, 2015 (and duplex after December 7, 2023)
- **One unit in a duplex or two-unit may not be used as STR for more than 30 days in a calendar year**

---

## Design Standards (§25-2-773(C))

- Porch open on 3 sides may project into front yard with roof
- Porch projecting into front yard must be ≥15 ft from front lot line
- Porch roof/overhang must be ≥13 ft from front lot line

---

## What Was Repealed / Removed

- §25-2-511 (Dwelling Unit Occupancy Limit): **Repealed**
- §25-2-774 (Two-Family Residential Use): **Repealed**
- §25-2-901 (Accessory Apartments): **Repealed**
- §25-2-841 (Group and Family Homes): **Deleted**
- Family Home, Group Home (Class I General/Limited, Class II) civic uses: **Deleted**
- Subchapter F applicability to Duplex Residential, Two-Family Residential, Family Homes, Group Homes: **Removed**
