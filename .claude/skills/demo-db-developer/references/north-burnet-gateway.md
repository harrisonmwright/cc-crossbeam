# North Burnet/Gateway (NBG) Density Bonus

## At a Glance
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| LDC Section | §25-2-657 |
| Ordinance | No. 20090924-130 (NBG Regulating Plan); amended Ord. 20130815-104 |
| Incentive Type | Density Bonus (North Burnet/Gateway Combining District) |
| Applicability | Properties within the North Burnet/Gateway combining district (-NBG), generally bounded by US-183 (Research Blvd), MoPac, Braker Lane, and Lamar Boulevard |

## Incentives Granted
| Incentive | Standard Value | Bonus Value |
|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| Maximum height | NBG base per zone (60–120 ft depending on NBG zone) | Up to 180 ft in certain NBG zones with max affordability |
| FAR | NBG base (2.0–4.0 per zone) | Up to 5.0–8.0 |
| Setbacks | NBG zone standards | Modified per NBG Regulating Plan zone requirements |
| Parking | NBG zone standards | Reductions available; some zones have parking maximums |

## Affordability Requirements

NBG uses a tiered bonus system based on affordability level:

| Tier | Set-aside % | Max Income | Height Bonus | FAR Bonus |
|------|------------|------------|--------------|-----------|
| Tier 1 | 10% of residential units | ≤80% MFI | +15 ft over base | +0.5 FAR |
| Tier 2 | 15% of residential units | ≤80% MFI (min 5% at ≤60% MFI) | +30 ft over base | +1.0 FAR |
| Tier 3 | 20% of residential units | ≤80% MFI (min 10% at ≤60% MFI) | Maximum bonus | Maximum FAR |

**Math check:** `affordable_units / total_residential_units ≥ tier_percentage`

For Tier 2 example: total affordable ≥ 15%, AND ≥ 5% of total units at ≤60% MFI.

Affordability period: 40 years for all tiers.
Fee-in-lieu: Available for Tier 1 only; Tier 2 and 3 require on-site units.

## Dimensional Standards Under Bonus
Dimensional standards are zone-specific within the NBG Regulating Plan. The following are illustrative ranges:

| NBG Zone | Base Height | Tier 1 Max | Tier 2 Max | Tier 3 Max |
|----------|-------------|------------|------------|------------|
| Gateway Commercial | 60 ft | 75 ft | 90 ft | 120 ft |
| Mixed Use Corridor | 60 ft | 75 ft | 90 ft | 120 ft |
| Town Center | 90 ft | 105 ft | 120 ft | 180 ft |
| Neighborhood Mixed Use | 40 ft | 55 ft | 70 ft | — |

*Confirm specific zone from the NBG Regulating Plan for the subject property.*

## Required Plan Elements
1. **Cover sheet:** "NBG Density Bonus" stated; LDC §25-2-657 cited; NBG zone identified; affordability tier declared
2. **Project data table:** Total residential units, affordable units, set-aside %, income limit(s), affordability period, tenure type, bonus tier
3. **Affordability schedule:** Each affordable unit by ID, MFI tier (with 60% MFI units separately identified for Tier 2/3)
4. **Site plan:** Height labeled; FAR calculation; NBG zone noted; setbacks per NBG zone
5. **Floor plans:** Affordable units labeled with MFI tier
6. **Elevations:** Maximum height from natural grade; story count

## Key Compliance Checks
- [ ] Property within NBG combining district (zoning includes -NBG)
- [ ] NBG zone identified on cover sheet
- [ ] LDC §25-2-657 cited
- [ ] Affordability tier declared (Tier 1, 2, or 3)
- [ ] Affordable count meets tier threshold — recompute from plan data
- [ ] For Tier 2/3: confirm split of 60% MFI vs. 80% MFI units
- [ ] Income limit correctly stated per tier
- [ ] Affordability period ≥ 40 years
- [ ] Proposed height ≤ tier maximum for NBG zone
- [ ] Proposed FAR ≤ tier maximum for NBG zone
- [ ] If Tier 2/3 and fee-in-lieu claimed: this is a FAIL (fee-in-lieu not available for Tier 2/3)
- [ ] Affordable units individually labeled on floor plans

## Notes
- NBG zone boundaries are defined in the NBG Regulating Plan; confirm which zone applies to the subject property before reviewing dimensional standards
- Some NBG zones have parking maximums in addition to minimums; review parking section of NBG Regulating Plan for the applicable zone
- If project is mixed-use (residential + commercial), only residential units count toward the affordable set-aside calculation
- Tier 2/3 require separate covenant documents for 60% MFI and 80% MFI affordable units; flag for [REVIEWER: verify separate covenants executed]
- Pedestrian realm standards (sidewalk width, street tree planting) apply in NBG zones; plan set should show compliance — flag for [REVIEWER: verify pedestrian realm standards]
