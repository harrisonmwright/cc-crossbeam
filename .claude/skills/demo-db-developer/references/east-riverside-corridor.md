# East Riverside Corridor (ERC) Density Bonus

## At a Glance
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| LDC Section | §25-2-653 |
| Ordinance | No. 20131017-049 (ERC Regulating Plan); amended Ord. 20161215-099 |
| Incentive Type | Density Bonus (East Riverside Corridor Combining District) |
| Applicability | Properties within the East Riverside Corridor combining district (-ERC), generally along East Riverside Drive between S. Lakeshore Blvd and Pleasant Valley Road |

## Incentives Granted
| Incentive | Standard Value | Bonus Value |
|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| Maximum height | ERC base (60 ft in most transect zones) | Up to 96 ft in certain transect zones with affordability |
| FAR | ERC base (2.0–3.0 depending on transect) | Up to 4.0–6.0 with density bonus |
| Setbacks | ERC transect standards | Not modified by density bonus; ERC transect governs |
| Parking | ERC standards | Reductions available per transect; some zones have parking maximums |

## Affordability Requirements
| Parameter | Rental | Ownership |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Set-aside % | 10% of total residential units | 10% of total residential units |
| Max income limit | ≤80% MFI | ≤80% MFI |
| Affordability period | 40 years | 40 years |
| Fee-in-lieu | Available at HCD-set rate |

**Math check:** `affordable_units / total_residential_units ≥ 0.10`

Alternative: Higher affordability (5% at ≤50% MFI) may substitute for standard 10% at 80% MFI in some transect zones.

## Dimensional Standards Under Bonus
The ERC is organized by transect zones (T3–T6). Bonus allowances vary by transect:

| Transect | Base Height | Bonus Height | Base FAR | Bonus FAR |
|----------|-------------|--------------|----------|-----------|
| T3 | 40 ft | 60 ft | 1.0 | 1.5 |
| T4 | 60 ft | 75 ft | 2.0 | 3.0 |
| T5 | 75 ft | 90 ft | 3.0 | 5.0 |
| T6 | 90 ft | 96 ft | 4.0 | 6.0 |

*Confirm transect zone for the specific property from the ERC Regulating Plan.*

## Required Plan Elements
1. **Cover sheet:** "East Riverside Corridor Density Bonus" stated; LDC §25-2-653 cited; ERC transect zone identified (T3–T6)
2. **Project data table:** Total residential units, affordable units, set-aside %, income limit, affordability period, tenure type; bonus FAR requested
3. **Affordability schedule:** Each affordable unit by ID, type, MFI tier, period
4. **Site plan:** Height labeled; FAR calculation shown; ERC transect zone noted; setbacks per ERC transect
5. **Floor plans:** Affordable units individually labeled
6. **Elevations:** Maximum height from natural grade shown; story count

## Key Compliance Checks
- [ ] Property within ERC combining district (zoning includes -ERC)
- [ ] ERC transect zone identified on cover sheet
- [ ] LDC §25-2-653 cited
- [ ] Affordable count ≥ 10% of residential units — recompute from plan data
- [ ] Income limit ≤ 80% MFI
- [ ] Affordability period ≥ 40 years
- [ ] Proposed height ≤ bonus height max for transect zone
- [ ] Proposed FAR ≤ bonus FAR max for transect zone
- [ ] Setbacks comply with ERC transect standards
- [ ] If fee-in-lieu: documentation present; do not compute amount
- [ ] Affordable units individually labeled on floor plans

## Notes
- The ERC Regulating Plan governs dimensional standards within the corridor; the LDC density bonus section provides affordability requirements layered on top
- Parking in ERC may have maximums (not just minimums) in transit-priority zones; verify parking count is neither too high nor too low
- Ground floor active use may be required in T5 and T6 transect zones for buildings along East Riverside Drive
- If project includes commercial/retail uses, verify ground floor use requirements per ERC Regulating Plan §6 (not captured in this reference)
- Compatibility standards may apply if lot is adjacent to single-family zoning; flag for [REVIEWER: verify compatibility standards]
